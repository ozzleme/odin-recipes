Bhindi Fry

Ingredients

  • 1kg bhindi
  • 7-8 garlic pods
  • 2-3 potatoes
  • mustard oil
  • salt
  • 1/2 tsp haldi
  • 2 big tsp dhaniya powder
  • 1 tsp lal mirch powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 lemon

How to Make:

  1. Chop bhindi and potatoes.
  2. Heat oil in pan. Add chopped garlic and stir fry until fragrant and slightly brown.
  3. Add chopped bhindi and stir fry on high flame mixing continuously. Do this until the bhindi starts to change colour.
  4. Add the potatoes and continue cooking on medium flame slightly covered so the potatoes start cooking.
  5. After 10-12 minutes open the lid and add all the masalas except garam masala.
  6. Continue mixing and stirring until the potatoes are completely cooked.
  7. Add the juice of half a lemon and keep cooking on low flame. This makes the bhindi crispy!
