Hummus

Ingredients

  • 200 grams chick peas
  • 4 big spoons tahini
  • 3-4 garlic
  • 1 lemon
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • baking soda
  • red chili powder

How to Make:

  1. Boil chick pears with pinch of salt and baking soda in a cooker.
  2. Take out the skin of the chickpeas.
  3. In a mixer, add the garlic and lemon first. Grind it.
  4. Add all the ingredients except olive oil, with a small amount of water. Grind it.
  5. Let it rest and settle.
  6. Grind again more gradually adding some olive oil to make it into a smooth paste.
  7. Garnish with olive oil and a small sprinkle of red chili powder.
