Palak Paneer

Description

This is a North Indian recipe that combines various masalas, spinach and paneer.

Ingredients

  • masala
  • 1 big onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 6-7 garlic pods
  • hari mirch
  • hari dhaniya
  • jeera
  • ghee
  • mustard oil
  • salt
  • 1 package paneer
  • 2 Kashmiri chillies

How to Make:

Palak Paste

  1. Wash the palak.
  2. Boil water and add palak. Boil for 3 minutes.
  3. Drain the water and keep aside. Wash palak with cold water or keep in cold water bath.
  4. Grind and make a paste of palak.

Masala

  1. Grind to a paste onions, tamoatoes, garlic, hari mirch, hari dhaniya, and jeera.

Paneer

  1. Cut paneer into cubes.
  2. Warm ghee in pan and cook on low heat for 5 minutes until slightly crispy.
  3. Drain ghee and set aside.

Gravy

  1. Heat ghee/oil in a pan.
  2. Add spinach.
  3. Add one small finely chopped onion to this.
  4. After the onion turns a little brown, add masala until the oil seperates.
  5. Now, add a little dhaniya powder and lal mirch powder.
  6. Once the masala is cooked add the palak paste and mix it well. Add water to adjust the consistency of the gravy.
  7. Add the paneer cubes
  8. Add garam masala powder and cream.
  9. Cook for a few minutes. Do not overcook as the palak is already cooked.

Tadka

  1. Take ghee and heat it.
  2. Chop chilies and add.
  3. Add to top of gravy and cover with lid quickly.
